# WDW Trip Itinerary + Scheduling Rules (Apr 5–11, 2026)

## Trip facts
- Resort: Disney’s Riviera Resort
- Party: 2 adults + 2 kids (ages 3 and 9)
- Style: half-day mornings + midday nap at Riviera

## Park day alignment (locked)
- Sun 4/5: Arrival
- Mon 4/6: EPCOT (Extended Evening Hours night)
- Tue 4/7: Animal Kingdom (earlier night)
- Wed 4/8: Magic Kingdom (Extended Evening Hours night)
- Thu 4/9: Resort / pool day (rest day)
- Fri 4/10: Hollywood Studios
- Sat 4/11: Depart

## Daily timing constraints (hard rules)
- Prefer leaving Riviera no earlier than 8:00am (7:30am is possible but not preferred).
- Be back to Riviera by ~1:00pm for the 3yo’s nap (target 12:30–1:00 arrival).
- Dinner window: flexible 4:30–6:30pm depending on whether we had a big brunch/lunch.
- Two late nights total: Monday (EPCOT) and Wednesday (MK). Avoid long/late dinners that block these.

## Dining preferences (hard constraints)
- Avoid buffets whenever possible.
- Avoid character dining and posed meet-and-greets (9yo dislikes them).
- OK: parades/cavalcades and low-pressure “wave-and-go” character exposure.
- We value theming + food quality; willing to travel for standout dining.
- Minimize transportation friction (especially TTC and long bus waits).

## Must-ride context (for park flow awareness)
- MK: thrills-dominant; guarantee TRON and/or Space Mountain using paid access; use Rider Switch.
- EPCOT: Guardians is a must (use VQ/paid strategy if applicable).
- HS: Rise + Slinky are musts (paid-first).
- AK: Flight of Passage + Everest are musts.
