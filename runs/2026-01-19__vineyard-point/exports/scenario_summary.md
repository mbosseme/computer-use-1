## Definitions

1. **Net Op Cash**: Rent - (Mtg + HOA + Tax + Ins + Maint + Mgmt).

2. **Opp Cost (Eq)**: 5% return on Upfront Cash (Down + Furnish + Closing).

3. **Avg Annual Econ**: Net Cash Cost + Opp Cost - (Sale Profit / Yrs).

4. **Premium (Econ)**: Avg Annual Econ - Rent Value of Vacation Time (Airbnb comp).

5. **Incr Benefit vs Empty**: How much cash renting saves vs keeping it empty (Net Cost Empty - Net Cost Rented).
