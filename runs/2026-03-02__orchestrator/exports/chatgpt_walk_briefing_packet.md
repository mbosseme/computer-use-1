## Copy/Paste Prompt for ChatGPT (recommended)
Use the following prompt verbatim in ChatGPT:

"You are helping me sharpen my executive operating context. I’m VP of Advanced Analytics and Supply Chain Technology at Premier. I’m giving you a context packet with active workstreams, risks, stakeholders, and open questions.

Act as an executive coach + chief of staff. Run a structured verbal interview with me in this order:
1) Priorities
2) Decisions
3) Risks
4) Stakeholders
5) Communication plan
6) Corrections to current assumptions

Rules:
- Ask one question at a time.
- Push for specificity: names, dates, decision owners, and escalation triggers.
- If I’m vague, ask a follow-up.
- Every 5 questions, summarize what you heard and list unresolved items.
- At the end, produce:
  A) Strategic priorities (2-week and 6-week)
  B) Decision ledger (decision, owner, due date, confidence)
  C) Risk ledger (risk, probability, impact, trigger, mitigation)
  D) Stakeholder action plan (who/why/when/message)
  E) Corrections to prior assumptions
  F) Open questions still unanswered

Do not be generic. Keep me focused and specific."

## High-Impact Questions to Answer During the Walk

### A) Priority hierarchy
1. What are the top 3 outcomes that matter most in the next 2 weeks?
2. What are the top 3 outcomes for the next 6 weeks?
3. Which current workstreams should be deliberately deprioritized right now?

### B) Decision clarity
4. Which decisions are already made but not yet consistently understood by teams?
5. Which decisions must be made this week to prevent drift?
6. For each pending decision, who is the final decision owner?

### C) Definitions and operating language
7. Which terms currently create confusion (examples: on/off/non-contract, representativeness, done criteria, velocity, scope)?
8. What is your preferred canonical definition for each?
9. Where should those definitions be published so all teams use the same language?

### D) Risk posture
10. What are the 5 highest risks right now?
11. What specific trigger should cause escalation for each risk?
12. What is your threshold for acceptable delay before executive intervention?

### E) Stakeholder map
13. Which stakeholders are mission-critical over the next 10 business days?
14. Where are relationships healthy vs fragile?
15. Who needs a direct touchpoint from you this week, and for what decision?

### F) Partner and governance
16. What is your desired posture with Solventum over the next 2 weeks?
17. What is your desired posture with Google/Pythian on governance/compliance/timing?
18. What cannot proceed without legal/compliance closure?

### G) Team operating model
19. What does “good execution” look like in the fusion model this month?
20. What anti-patterns are appearing (ownership gaps, meeting overload, duplicate analysis, unclear handoffs)?
21. What is your explicit rule for when work should be paused, reassigned, or escalated?

### H) Communication design
22. What messages should be delivered to leadership this week?
23. What messages should be delivered to delivery teams this week?
24. What messages should be delivered to partners this week?

### I) Corrections to current context
25. What are the top assumptions in current briefings that are wrong or incomplete?
26. What context is missing that would materially change prioritization?
27. What should the orchestrator stop doing, start doing, and continue doing?
